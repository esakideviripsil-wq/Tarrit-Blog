# Printed Shirts for Men — A 2026 Style & Buying Guide (India)

A quick reference for choosing and wearing printed shirts for men.

## Priorities
- **Pattern:** small, tonal, low-contrast motifs over loud prints
- **Fabric:** premium Giza cotton for a refined finish
- **Styling:** one printed piece per outfit, everything else solid
- **Scale:** smaller motifs read more versatile

## Avoid
- Oversized, high-contrast prints
- Synthetic fabrics that flatten colour
- Pairing a print with another pattern
