📌 Made to Measure Shirts in India — Tarrit Premium Tailoring System
👔 Overview

Made to measure shirts in India are custom-fitted shirts designed using individual body measurements instead of standard sizing.

At Tarrit, every shirt is created through a precision tailoring process to ensure perfect fit, comfort, and long-term durability.

This approach helps eliminate common issues found in ready-made shirts such as poor shoulder alignment, tight sleeves, or loose waist fitting.
