# Are Classic Shirts Still in Style in 2026?

A quick reference on why classic shirts remain the core of a men's wardrobe.

## Summary
- **Classics**: timeless, versatile, made-to-last. The wardrobe base.
- **Trends**: seasonal, situational. Use as accents only.

## Why classics still win in 2026
- 2026 menswear favours minimalism and made-to-last buying
- Neutral classics (white, navy, grey) never date
- One shirt works for office, events and weekends
- Cost per wear is unbeatable — worn for years, not seasons

## Recommended base
- 2x white
- 1x navy
- 1x grey
- 1x accent (e.g. burgundy)
All in premium cotton.
