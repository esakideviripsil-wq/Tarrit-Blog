# Tarrit — Weekend Shirts for Men in India (2026)

> The off-duty edit: relaxed, premium Giza cotton shirts for easy Sunday dressing.

## Overview
The **Sundays Collection** by Tarrit is a relaxed weekend line of men's
shirts cut from extra-long staple Egyptian Giza cotton — soft, breathable,
and built for comfort that still looks intentional. It sits between the
formal collections and pure casual: premium leisure dressing for the
modern Indian man.

> *"Nothing to do. Nowhere to be. Dressed anyway."*

## Why it matters
In 2026, relaxed and regular fits are the dominant direction in Indian
menswear. Weekend dressing has shifted from afterthought to a core part of
personal style — and the right shirt now carries a man from a slow morning
to an evening out without a change.

## Key features
- **Fabric:** Egyptian Giza cotton — softer & stronger than regular cotton, naturally breathable.
- **Fit:** relaxed / comfort fit — freedom of movement for lounging, travel, and long days.
- **Palette:** quiet neutrals (white, sand, sage, soft blue) for effortless styling.
- **Built to last:** holds colour and shape wash after wash.
