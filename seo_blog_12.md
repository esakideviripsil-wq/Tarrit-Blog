# Solid vs Printed Shirts for Men

A quick reference on choosing between solid and printed shirts.

## Summary
- **Solid shirts**: versatile, formal-ready, easy to re-wear. The wardrobe base.
- **Printed shirts**: occasional, casual, memorable — use as accents only.

## When to pick solid
- Office, interviews, formal events
- Daily wear where you repeat outfits often
- Building a capsule wardrobe

## Recommended base
White, navy, grey, burgundy solid color shirts cover most needs.
