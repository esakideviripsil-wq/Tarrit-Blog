# Men's Shirt Fit Guide (India, 2026)

A quick reference comparing the three shirt fits Indian men shop for, and which suits casual weekend wear.

## The three fits

| Fit | Comfort | Structure | Best for |
|-----|---------|-----------|----------|
| Regular | Moderate | Classic | Traditional looks |
| Comfort | Very High | Loose | Travel, long wear |
| Relaxed | High | Balanced | Modern weekends |

## Verdict

For most men building a 2026 weekend wardrobe, **relaxed fit** wins — it combines comfort with enough structure to still look sharp. Fabric quality matters as much as the cut: a relaxed shirt in premium cotton holds its shape; a cheap one doesn't.
