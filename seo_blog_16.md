# Subtle vs Bold Jacquard Patterns for Everyday Wear

A quick reference on choosing jacquard patterns for daily use.

## Summary
- **Subtle / tone-on-tone**: versatile, repeatable, smart-casual. The everyday base.
- **Bold paisley / floral**: situational, memorable. Best as occasion pieces.

## Why subtle wins for everyday
- Pairs with almost anything
- Can be worn weekly without standing out
- Reads as understated and refined

## When to go bold
- Events, celebrations, evenings out
- When presence matters more than repetition
