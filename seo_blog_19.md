# Tarrit — Tencel Shirts for Men in India (2026)

> The sustainable luxury edit: premium, breathable Tencel shirts built for India's climate.

## Overview
The **100% Tencel Collection** by Tarrit is a conscious-luxury line of men's
shirts made from Tencel — a premium fibre spun from sustainably sourced wood
pulp in a closed-loop process. Softer than cotton, more breathable, and fully
biodegradable: luxury with a conscience.

## Why Tencel
- **Breathable + moisture-wicking** — stays cool and dry in heat and humidity.
- **Odour-resistant** — stays fresher for longer through the day.
- **Fast-drying** — a real advantage through the monsoon.
- **Soft, fluid drape** — a premium, silk-like hand-feel.
- **Sustainable** — closed-loop production, biodegradable fibre.

## Tencel vs the alternatives
| Fabric | Best for | Trade-off |
|--------|----------|-----------|
| Tencel | Humid climate, all-day comfort, sustainability | Gentle care needed |
| Giza cotton | Crisp formal structure | Slightly warmer |
| Linen | Breezy casual days | Creases heavily |
| Synthetic | Low price | Traps heat, not biodegradable |
