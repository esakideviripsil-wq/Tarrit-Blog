# Pre-Wedding Shoot Shirts for Men — Guide for Grooms (India, 2026)

A quick reference for choosing pre-wedding shoot shirts for men that photograph well.

## Priorities
- **Fabric:** Premium Giza cotton — breathable, structured, low glare
- **Fit:** Structured, comfortable in seated and candid poses
- **Colour:** The best shirt colours for photography are muted / tonal, not bright
- **Texture:** Subtle jacquard for depth without noise

## Avoid
- Stark white in harsh sunlight
- Synthetic blends (heat + wrinkles)
- Oversized fits and loud patterns
